Intel® Euclid(TM) Developer Kit
The Intel® Euclid(TM) Developer Kit features the integration of Intel® RealSense(TM) camera technologies with Intel® Atom(TM) x7-Z8700 CPU to produce an all-in-one compute/depth camera device in a compact and sleek pacakge. The Intel Euclid Developer Kit is designed to be operable out of he box with pre-installed software including Linux® operating system.
Kit includes:
  • Integrated compute/camera platform with integrated graphics, 4GB memory, 32GB storage, USB3.0 port, HDMI video output, Wi-Fi® and Bluetooth®. The camera capability includes depth sensing, wide-angle fisheye camera and inertial measurement unit
  • Battery pack for mobile use
  • Power adapter (US)
  • USB cable
  • HDMI cable
